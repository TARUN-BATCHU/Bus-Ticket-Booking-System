How to book bus tickets online on Our website Each and every booking or transaction you do on the AbhiBus website is simple, safe & secure. Enter the Source and Destination details in the “leaving from” and “Going to” tab respectively Select the “Journey date” from calendar icon and click on the “Search” button Select any bus operator of your choice from the list of buses which are displayed Select a seat, boarding and dropping points and hit “Continue to payment” Enter all the required information including contact number & passenger details Complete the payment process be selecting your mode of payment Once the payment process is done, you will receive an M-ticket on your mobile as SMS, whatsapp message and an e-ticket on email ID that was entered under “Enter Contact Details.”
